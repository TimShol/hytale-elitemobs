---
title: Effects
description: Configure tier-based elite effects.
---

Effects are defined in `effects.yml` under `byId`.
Effects are applied per elite tier based on `isEnabledPerTier`.

## Common Fields

- `isEnabled` toggle
- `isEnabledPerTier` per-tier toggles
- `amountMultiplierPerTier` for effect strength
- `templates` for effect asset templates
Templates control the underlying entity effect assets.

## Example: Projectile Resistance

```yaml
byId:
  projectile_resistance:
    amountMultiplierPerTier: [0.0, 0.0, 0.0, 0.7, 0.85]
    isEnabled: true
    isEnabledPerTier: [false, false, false, true, true]
```
